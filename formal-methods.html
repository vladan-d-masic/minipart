No. Country Methods Status Description of the method Category
1 Albania Public meetings (hearings) yes The authorities in charge of drafting the planning documents organize one or more public hearings prior to any decision-making related to the planning documents at the central level and the overall local plans and repeat them in view of fully informing the stakeholders and settling possible conflicts. Info on the place, date and time of each public meeting and drafts of planning documents available at least 30 days prior to the meetings. Public meeting is held with a wide public, which represents all layers and profiles of the society that live or work within the planned territory. Public remarks are accepted or rejected with explanation. Consultation
Albania Consultations yes The authorities in charge of drafting the planning documents organize one or more public hearings and consultations prior to any decision-making related to the planning documents at the central level and the overall local plans and repeat them in view of fully informing the stakeholders and settling possible conflicts. Info on the place, date and time of each public meeting and drafts of planning documents available at least 30 days prior to the meetings. Advisory meetings are organised with interest groups and experts in the field, and with local advisory forums (FKV). Advisory meetings focus on soliciting opinions and suggestions from specialized groups or those directly affected by the planning document. Public remarks are accepted or rejected with explanation. Consultation
Albania Notifications - newspapers or other media yes Announcement on the place, date and time of each public meeting and the drafts of planning documents at least 30 days prior to the meetings in two newspapers with the largest circulation. summaries of the observations and proposals during the public meetings. Also publishes summaries of the observations and proposals during the public meetings. Information
Albania Notifications - registry yes At least 30 days prior to meetings publishes information related to the planning documents, including the summaries on the co-ordination, the observations, proposals and conclusions reached. Also publishes summaries of public meetings within 10 days. Information
Albania Forum for Local Counselling /Local advisory forums / citizen advisory panels no Voluntary association of community representatives, organized in support of the interests of the community to consult with the municipality regarding decision- making on the main issues and developments. It can be institutionalised through agreement with a head of the local unit. The consultation with the FKV and the public meeting can be organized separately or coordinated. Information
