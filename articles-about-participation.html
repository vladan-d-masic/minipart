Welcome to our search page for publications on participation! You can search publications by keywords, country, and method. The “Methods” dropdown menu includes terms that refer not only to methods, but also to approaches, techniques, and communication channels used in public participation processes in decision-making. A detailed categorisation of methods, along with their descriptions, can be found on the “Formal Methods” and “Informal Methods” pages.
